What happens during a CatsDog session?

Oodles of fun creating and capturing priceless memories

Together, we’ll head out on an adventure with your fur-baby to find the most beautiful and unique spots to strike a pose, roll in the grass and if your dog feels like it, take a paddle in the water! Rest assured, most of the furry subjects we work with have wiggly bums that don’t stay still for long, but we have plenty of tricks up our sleeves to make them look like a seasoned pros in front of the camera. We make the sessions fun and interactive for your dog – your job is simply to relax – the more you enjoy yourself the more your pooch will too. You might even get a chance to play second assistant! Where possible you can be involved in throwing the ball or holding your fur-kid on the lead while we get creative.

Sessions are preferably booked mid week when public areas are quieter as Saturday availability is very limited. *

Where would my session take place?

On location somewhere striking – the choice is yours!

CatsDog sessions take place on location, somewhere your pet is relaxed and can be themselves. If there is somewhere special you have in mind for your session we’d love to hear it. Open-minded? We regularly shoot at various locations across the North West, from utterly urban to cracking countryside spots. On booking we can recommend to you the area we will be covering when you’d like the session to take place and help match up your dream location. We also have a location guide map to help you decide – available on request

What happens to my images after the session?

Choose the moments for us to craft into tangible pieces of art.

We carefully select the very best images from your session and polish them up for your image premiere. The images you see at the presentation will be 80% complete, enhanced to make your fur-kid look their absolute best. Any more complicated jobs like lead removal or head stitches will be done once you make your order. You will be invited to a private viewing appointment (arranged prior to the session taking place) at our Preston studio – it’s a bit like going to the cinema, but starring your fur-kid! Together we will munch on some choccys with a hot drink and enjoy a slideshow of your session. This is many of our client’s favourite part, though that could be something to do with the chocolates! After joyfully selecting your top images, we'll design something that perfectly reflects your individual style and taste. In the studio we have several examples of the beautiful wall art products we offer for you to check out, you'll be pleased to hear they are among the finest in the industry.

Once you have made your selection, all that’s left to do is sit back while we do the work and look forward to your wall art’s arrival. When your finished artwork arrives, we guarantee you will instantly fall in love!
